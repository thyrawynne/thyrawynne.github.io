University

I'm an undergraduate student at Universitas Sam Ratulangi, pursuing a degree in Information and Technology. The 2nd year has been a rollercoaster of experiences and exciting discoveries in the world of IT.

From carrying our laptops to college every day to wrestling with code, the challenges are as rewarding as they are stimulating. The journey has ignited a passion for technology that grows stronger with every line I write.

Technology Trends

  • Artificial Intelligence (AI): AI is rapidly evolving, with applications that are transforming fields like healthcare, finance, and manufacturing. It's fascinating to see how AI is pushing the boundaries of what's possible.
  • Augmented Reality (AR) and Virtual Reality (VR): These technologies are creating immersive experiences that blur the lines between the physical and digital worlds. AR and VR have the potential to revolutionize entertainment, education, and even surgery.
  • Internet of Things (IoT): The IoT connects everyday devices to the internet, allowing them to collect and share data. This interconnectedness is leading to smarter homes, cities, and industries.
  • Blockchain: This secure and distributed ledger technology underpins cryptocurrencies like Bitcoin. But its potential goes beyond finance. Blockchain's ability to create a tamper-proof record of transactions has applications in supply chain management, voting, and more.
